OUR SERVICES

E-waste recycling

This includes the collection, transportation, sorting, and recycling of various types of electronic waste, such as computers, laptops, printers, mobile phones, and other electronic devices.

Li battery waste management

This includes the collection, transportation, and disposal of various types of lithium-ion batteries, such as those used in mobile phones, laptops, and electric vehicles.

Secure data destruction

This includes the secure destruction of data stored on electronic devices to prevent any sensitive information from falling into the wrong hands.

Compliance with environmental regulations

This includes compliance with local and national environmental regulations related to e-waste and battery waste disposal and recycling.

Customized solutions

This includes the development of customized e-waste and battery waste management solutions to meet the specific needs of individual clients.

Sustainability consulting

This includes providing advice and recommendations to clients on how to improve their sustainability practices related to e-waste and battery waste management.

Awareness and education programs

This includes conducting awareness and education programs for clients, employees, and the general public on the importance of responsible e-waste and battery waste management practices.

IT asset disposition (ITAD)

This includes the secure and environmentally-friendly disposal of end-of-life IT assets, such as servers, data storage devices, and networking equipment, to ensure that they are not contributing to e-waste.

Product destruction

This includes the secure destruction of defective or recalled products to prevent them from entering the secondary market and potentially causing harm to consumers or the environment.
